Our Pennsylvania Banquet Hall

 

Our beautiful Pennsylvania Banquet Hall is conveniently located on Park Avenue in Quakertown, Bucks County Pa.

 

The Hall Includes:

 

Four (4) hour affair with seating for up to 180 people.

Large round tables and chairs with table covers.

Elevated stage with a skirted head table.

Skirted buffet tables with elegant chaffing stands.

Gift table with hand-made mailbox for envelopes.

  CO2 system and taps at the bar; Ample refrigeration.

 

Ample room for dance floor and DJ.

  High hats with dimmers, artificial trees with lights.

 

Waitresses, bartender, full set up and clean up.

 

All of these are included for the unbelievably low price of only

 

$450 on Saturdays

Minimum 60 people

 

$400 on Fridays & Sundays

Minimum 50 people

Please choose any of our Party Plans with your hall rental.  You are also required to purchase our unlimited beverage plan which includes coffee, tea, sodas, juices, mixers, ice, and cups for $3.00 per person.  You may provide your own alcohol/beer/wine which our bartenders will serve.  No cash bar permitted.

 

Deposit required to hold any date.  Minimum guest count and menu selections due 10 days prior to affair;

Balance due day before event.  Please add 6% PA sales tax and 20% service charge.

Gratuities not included. One hour overtime is $250. Reduced price for weekday events.

C&C Catering is the exclusive caterer for the Banquet Hall. No outside food permitted. Own cake is permitted.

 

Absolutely no confetti or glitter permitted in any way (including decoration items such as flowers, banners, etc.)
