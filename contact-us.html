Helpful Tips

  • While we appreciate 7 days notice, last minute orders are welcome.

 

  • Tables should be covered with a tablecloth and aluminum foil.  Sternos must sit in chafing dish racks only.  We are not responsible for any damage caused by sternos.

 

  • Cold cups, coffee urns, cake plates and forks, and tablecovers are not supplied.

 

  • Food must not be placed in direct sunlight or be kept outside for an extended period of time.  Food will not heat properly in a breezy area or under air conditioning vents.

 

  • A 25% deposit is required on all orders.  All payment must be made in cash, certified check, money order or credit card.

 

  • Racks and waterpans must be returned within 30 days. Nominal deposit on equipment.

 

  • All trays of food are half of a chafing dish rack.  Two trays make one full rack.

 

  • Gluten Free meals available

 

  • Full payment is due upon delivery.  Refunds are by credit slip only (72 hour notice required).  Parties canceled without a minimum of 72 hours notice will forfeit deposit.

 

  • Experienced wait service available. $40 per hour, minimum 4 hours; Subject to availability; provided on a first come, first serve basis; Gratuities not included.

 

  • Immediate attention given for bereavements.

 

  • All catering subject to sales tax; prices subject to change.

 

  • Delivery throughout New York, New Jersey, eastern Pennsylvania, and Southern Connecticut.

 

  • Delivery charge may apply in your area.

 

  • For your convenience we accept Visa, Master Card and American Express; please note there is a 2% surcharge on all charges over $400.
